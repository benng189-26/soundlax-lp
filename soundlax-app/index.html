Meet SoundLax

SoundLax is a sound app built for creating background noise to help you focus, rest, and sleep.

Our philosophy is simple: the internet already asks for enough attention. SoundLax was designed to ask for less and only focus on bringing the carefully curated sounds and music for you to dive into your space. No daily streaks, no AI audio meditation guides.

Not try to have the biggest sound library on earth, but good sounds and music we actually enjoy leaving on for hours.

what makes   SoundLax different

Designed with intention, not excess.

quick access

We work on SoundLax to reduce the friction when you're using the app. You should be able to start instantly, open the app, press play, and that's it.

sounds are the heart of SoundLax

We spent way too much time testing sounds to make sure they actually feel good to listen to for hours. And we hope you do.

built for distracted brains

You're not alone. SoundLax was made by someone who understands the struggle of trying to focus.

100% focus guarantee

We asked family and friends to test SoundLax while working. 100% said it worked. (They claimed: no bias).
