Meet SoundLax

SoundLax is a sound app built for creating background noise to help you focus, rest, and sleep.

Our philosophy is simple: the internet already asks for enough attention. SoundLax was designed to ask for less and only focus on bringing the carefully curated sounds and music for you to dive into your space. No daily streaks, no AI audio meditation guides.

Not try to have the biggest sound library on earth, but good sounds and music we actually enjoy leaving on for hours.

frequently asked questions

What is SoundLax?

SoundLax is a minimalist sound environment app designed for focus, relaxation, and sleep.

The goal is simple: less friction, better atmospheres, and quicker access to the sounds you actually want to hear.

Does SoundLax work offline?

Short answer: Yes. Some sounds may require an internet connection depending on how they are streamed or updated. Offline support may expand over time.

How does Premium work?

SoundLax Premium unlocks:

  • Unlimited listening
  • Premium sound environments
  • Curated atmosphere mixes
  • Sleep-focused environments
  • Living backgrounds
  • Future sound updates

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID account.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

You can manage subscriptions anytime in:

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

I found a bug or issue

Please go to Setting and find Help and Support.

Will more sounds be added?

Yep, but carefully.

We want SoundLax to grow slowly with high-quality environments and curated sound spaces, without becoming overwhelming or cluttered.
