About Bentoji

Small studio. Direct collaboration. No unnecessary layers.

Bentoji is the independent studio of Ben Nguyen, a digital designer with over a decade of experience creating products, websites and digital experiences across New Zealand and Australia.

After years working with agencies and product teams, Bentoji was created to offer something simpler: working directly with the designer who actually builds the work.

Whether it's designing a new product, improving an existing experience, or creating a brand from scratch, every project stays focused on clarity, usability and thoughtful execution.
