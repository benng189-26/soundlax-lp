Privacy Policy for SoundLax

Effective date · 19 June 2026

SoundLax (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the SoundLax mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information we collect

We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

a. Personal information

We do not require users to create an account to use SoundLax. We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it, for example when contacting us for support.

b. Usage data

We may collect limited, non-personal information about how the App is used, including:

  • App interactions (such as features used and session duration)
  • Device model and operating system
  • App version
  • General performance and crash reports

This information helps us improve the performance, stability, and overall user experience of SoundLax.

c. App preferences

SoundLax stores certain preferences locally on your device to provide a personalised experience. These may include:

  • Selected sounds, music and playlists
  • Volume and mixer settings
  • Timer and sleep settings
  • Focus session preferences
  • App blocking preferences
  • Premium feature settings

Unless otherwise stated, this information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-party services

We use trusted third-party services to help operate and improve SoundLax. These may include:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Firestore
  • Google Cloud Storage

These services may collect anonymous or aggregated usage information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not control how these third parties process your information.

3. In-app purchases

SoundLax offers premium features through in-app purchases. All purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, process, or store your payment information.

4. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Understand how users interact with features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features and enhancements
  • Improve the overall user experience

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

5. App blocking permissions

SoundLax includes optional Focus features that can help reduce distractions by blocking selected apps during a focus session. These features use Apple’s Screen Time APIs and require your explicit permission before they can function.

SoundLax does not monitor the content you access, collect information about how you use other apps, or transmit your app usage data to our servers.

6. Data storage and security

Most preferences are stored locally on your device. Where cloud services such as Firebase are used, data is transmitted and stored securely using industry-standard encryption.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to certain data processing where applicable

Because SoundLax does not require user accounts, we may not always be able to associate anonymous usage information with an individual user. If you have any privacy-related requests, please contact us using the details below.

8. International data transfers

Some third-party service providers may process data on servers located outside your country of residence. These providers are responsible for protecting your information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

9. Children’s privacy

SoundLax is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Effective Date” at the top of this policy will be updated accordingly.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us: soundlax.studio@gmail.com

12. Consent

By downloading or using SoundLax, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
