Privacy Policy

Last updated · June 2026

SoundLax is a sound environment app made by Bentoji Studio. We keep things simple: the app is built to help you focus, relax, and sleep - not to collect your data. This policy explains what little information is involved and how it's handled.

What we collect

SoundLax is designed to work without an account. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or contacts through the app itself.

  • On-device settings: your preferences (volumes, favourites, last-played sounds) are stored on your device.
  • Purchases & subscriptions: handled entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We never see your payment details.
  • Anonymous diagnostics: aggregated, non-identifying crash and performance data may be used to improve stability.

What we don't do

  • No selling or sharing of personal data.
  • No third-party advertising or ad tracking.
  • No building of personal profiles.

Email & contact

If you email us or join our updates list, we use your email only to reply or to send the updates you asked for. You can opt out at any time.

Children

SoundLax is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email soundlax.studio@gmail.com.
